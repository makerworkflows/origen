What We Do

Four Pillars of Heritage Recovery

From uncovering your lineage to securing your second passport — we handle the research, the records, and the paperwork so you can focus on what it means.

📜

Full Genealogy

Deep-line ancestry research across civil, church, immigration, and military records. We trace your family back as far as the records allow.

  • Single-line research (paternal or maternal)
  • Dual-line comprehensive packages
  • International record retrieval
  • Family tree documentation and narrative report
  • DNA analysis integration
📷

Digital Document Preservation

High-resolution scanning and archival of family photographs, vital records, letters, and heirlooms. Preserving what time cannot.

  • Standard and fragile document scanning
  • Photo restoration and enhancement
  • Cloud-based family archive
  • Organized digital cataloging
  • Family archive sessions (up to 100 items)
🛂

Citizenship by Descent

Eligibility assessment, lineage documentation, and full application packages for citizenship by descent through European ancestry.

  • Eligibility assessment and pathway mapping
  • Vital record collection (birth, marriage, death, naturalization)
  • Translation and legalization
  • Full lineage documentation packages
  • Attorney referrals for consulate filing
📎

Apostille Services

Document authentication and apostille processing for international use. Required for citizenship applications, foreign legal proceedings, and overseas transactions.

  • Federal and state apostille processing
  • Document authentication for all 50 states
  • Embassy and consulate legalization
  • Certified translation coordination
  • Rush processing available

How It Works

From Question to Confirmation

01

Discovery Call

We learn what you know about your ancestry, identify the most promising pathways, and outline a clear scope of work. Free, no obligation.

02

Research & Recovery

We locate, retrieve, and verify the vital records that build your lineage — birth certificates, marriage records, immigration documents, naturalization files.

Records are organized, translated where needed, apostilled for international use, and compiled into a complete application-ready package.
